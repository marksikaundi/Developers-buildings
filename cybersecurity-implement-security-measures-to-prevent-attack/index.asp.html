Description

The today’s digital world is characterized by risks; new risks emerge not only every day but every hour of that day. Connecting to the internet increases the possibilities of hackers targeting individuals and organizations. Our professional and personal lives have begun gravitating toward the internet world, mobile computing, and multimedia. This widespread phenomenon has increased the vulnerability to malicious attacks, fraud, invasions of privacy, and others such unpleasantries.

Cyber security helps with saving individuals and businesses from cyber criminals, hackers, and other fraud agents. However, the questions are how good is your knowledge about cyber security? Can identify different types of cyber threats? Do you know what the security measures and solutions are suitable to be used with the different types of cyber threats? This free course module - “Cyber security: Understand Threats and Prevent Attacks”- which is considered one of the most important workplace essentials will provide you with the basic knowledge and skills that will help you answer the above-mentioned questions. You will be able to identify the different types of cyber threats as well as implement the suitable measures to prevent or control any cyberattack you or your organization may face.

LinCademy courses are designed differently as they:

  • are concise. content is presented right to the point without side talks. You will get huge amount of knowledge and develop many skills in a short time.
  • have professional and high-quality instructional videos that includes expressive graphics, and
  • knowledge is supported by animated case studies that will help you reflect what you have learnt on real life situations.

What Will I Get ?

  • Understand cyber security fundamentals
  • Differentiate between types of malwares
  • Understand what security breaches are
  • Identify security breaches forms
  • Identify cyberattacks types
  • Identify prevention methods against cyberthreats
  • Set a strong password
  • Protect your Smartphone
  • Apply techniques of social networks security
  • Protect your organization against critical cyberthreats
  • Develop effective prevention methods
  • Identify prevention software against cyberthreats

Requirements

  • No prerequisites required except for strong determination to develop cyber security awareness skills.
