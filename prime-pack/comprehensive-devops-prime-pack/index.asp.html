About the Prime Pack:

DevOps demand has grown 40–45 percent over the last 4 years and still, it is one of the highly paid profiles. The highest reported salary for a DevOps engineer ranges between $45000 to $58000. 

Tutorials Point brings you our best collection of courses where you can learn all about DevOps right from scratch to be a full-time professional. 

In this prime pack, we will start with understanding the course of Developing Concepts and different applications of Containers (Docker). Then we will move towards implementing Build Automation & CI/CD Pipeline using Jenkins, Ansible, Docker, and Kubernetes. Once we are familiar with that, we deep dive into understanding the implementation of  Cloud IaaS (AWS or Azure) and Container Orchestration. Later on, we move towards practical implementation of Ansible Automation and Python Scripting. By the end of this prime pack, you will be guided to developing 15+ projects that will help you with understanding the practical implementation of DevOps concepts and features.
