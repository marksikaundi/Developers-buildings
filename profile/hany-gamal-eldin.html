Classical Guitar Essentials

Hi, I am Hany Gamal. I am glad that you found me among the thousands of websites out there!
I am a full-time guitar teacher since 2006. I studied Classical Guitar with ABRSM, that’s Associated Board of the Royal School of Music, and I finished Grade 8 in classical guitar in 2004.

My real passion is teaching, people keep telling me that I am such a good teacher. So I took their advice and started making courses online. 

Little that I know that it will change my life, and I will have more than 15000 students across 30 countries!

I have a best-seller course on Udemy.com and my courses ranked among the top 2% on coursemarks.com
