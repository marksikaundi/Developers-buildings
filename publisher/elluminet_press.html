Elluminet Press is a publisher with more than 200 books in print, beautifully bound in either hardback or paperback, as well as in electronic formats, covering a variety of genres. Our vision has always been to develop resources that break down complex subjects into easy-to-follow textbooks that people can understand. We have developed textbooks for academic customers all over the world in countries including the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, South Africa and the United States. Colleges and Universities have integrated our Microsoft Office, Computer Hardware, Google Docs, HTML and Python guides into their curriculums.
