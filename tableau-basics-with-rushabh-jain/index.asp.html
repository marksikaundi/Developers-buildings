Hola !!

Welcome to my course on Tableau Basics. I have been training more than 2500+ Students now on how to begin your journey on creating interactive dashboard and story lines using this Super powerful tool.

The highlights of this course are :

  1. · Begin from scratch by solving a real-world analytical challenge using Tableau

  2. · Learn how to import your data in Tableau from MS Excel, Pdfs, MySQL and 30 More Sources.

  3. · Easily create a wide range of INTERACTIVE GRAPHS and VISUALISATIONS using drag and drop technology.

  4. · Learn to create POWERFUL reports, dashboards and storyline in Tableau with a few mouse clicks.

  5. · Gain valuable INSIGHTS and INTELLIGENCE from your business, finance and marketing data in a FRACTION of the time of traditional spreadsheets

  6. · Use ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE with No Prior Knowledge to make your Dashboards give you key insights on it’s own.

  7. · PUBLISH reports and dashboards on the Internet and view using laptops, tablets, or smartphones in minutes.

  8. · FAQ on items which are generally questions from my past teaching experience

  9. · RECAP after completion of every section of learning so that you are always updated/ ready incase you need to brush up for your interviews
